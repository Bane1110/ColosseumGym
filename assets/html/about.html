Our Company and Our Goals

Colosseum Gym opened its doors in 2002. And from that time on it made a whole lot of changes. We started in Serbia, Belgrade where we became very popular and therefore we expanded on the whole Balkan. Not long after that, we started opening gym all over Europe. And in 2009 we became best rated gym in Europe. Then we started investing in over the sea places like USA which became a hotspot for industry and popularity. Now days we are very close to opening gyms all over Asia and South America.
Our goals are to make gym affordable for people all over the world and to make the world a lot healthier. We already lower obesity in USA by 5% and we hope that number will go up to 20% in the next 5 years.
Every gym is devided in halls for every type of training be it Powerlifting, Bodybuilding, Weightlifting, Crossfit or Cardio. Make sure you visit us!
